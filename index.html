FAQ

Common Questions

What does "fully on-chain" mean? +
Every camel image is generated by the smart contract itself. No external servers, no IPFS, no off-chain dependencies. As long as Ethereum exists, your camel exists.
How much does it cost? +
Free mint: 4,500 camels available, 1 per wallet. Just pay gas. Paid mint: 0.00015 ETH per camel, up to 25 per wallet.
How many can I mint? +
1 free mint per wallet. After that, you can mint up to 25 paid camels per wallet.
What's the royalty? +
5% on secondary sales (ERC-2981 standard). Supported by major marketplaces.
Which wallets are supported? +
Any Ethereum wallet works — MetaMask, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.
