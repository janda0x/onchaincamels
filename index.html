// 04 — FAQ
FREQUENTLY ASKED
What is OnChain Camels?
OnChain Camels is a collection of 5,000 generative pixel art camels fully stored on the Ethereum blockchain. No IPFS, no servers — the art lives on-chain forever.
How does the free mint work?
The first 4,500 camels are claimable for free — you only pay gas. Limited to 1 per wallet address. Connect your wallet and click Free Mint.
What wallet do I need?
Any Ethereum wallet that supports EIP-1193 works — MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, etc. Make sure you are on Ethereum Mainnet.
Where can I view my NFT?
After minting, your camel will appear on OpenSea within a few minutes. You can also call tokenURI() directly on the contract to get the on-chain SVG.
How many traits are there?
Each camel has 8 traits: Body Color (10), Background (8), Pose (4), Hump (2), Accessory (8), Saddle (5), Mood (7), and Extra (6) — resulting in 134 million+ possible combinations.
